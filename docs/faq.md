# FAQ

**Does it identify people or cars?** No. Only aggregate counters exist.
Faces and plates are pixelated during analysis; no embeddings are computed.

**Is it enforcement?** No. It imposes nothing on anyone. Enforcement belongs
to competent authorities (in Poland: Policja / GITD) with certified devices.

**What exactly is stored?** Per-minute counters, observed seconds per
interval, and camera health events. No frames, no video, no personal data.

**Why do charts show gaps?** Because honesty: when the camera pool did not
observe the crossing, the interval renders as "no data" — never as zero.

**Can it measure speed?** Only as a screening indicator, and time-based
conflict metrics (PET/TTC) require a calibrated camera. Never evidence.

**What data does the live demo use?** Fully synthetic data.
