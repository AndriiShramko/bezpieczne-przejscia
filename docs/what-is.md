# What is Bezpieczne Przejścia / SafeCross?

Bezpieczne Przejścia (Polish for "safe crossings") is a privacy-preserving
computer-vision demonstrator that converts a camera view of one pedestrian
crossing into anonymous aggregate safety metrics. It answers questions like
"how many pedestrians cross per hour", "how often do drivers fail to yield",
and "what share of pedestrians walk head-down" — as trends, without ever
identifying a person or vehicle.

It is a demonstrator of competence (analytics + privacy engineering),
not an enforcement product. All published data is synthetic.
