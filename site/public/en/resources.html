Path to decision-makers

  • National road-safety council — target −50% by 2030; we provide a scalable method aligned with Safe System.
  • City road authority — a ranking of the most dangerous crossings and budget justification.
  • Mayor / city council — resident safety as a visible win + grant-readiness.
  • National roads / enforcement / police crash DB — objective behavioural data that's missing today.
